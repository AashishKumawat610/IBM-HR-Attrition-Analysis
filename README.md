ğŸ” Key Insights
 Overall attrition rate is 16.12%, meaning nearly 1 in 6 employees left the organization.
 Sales and R&D departments contribute the highest attrition, with Sales showing disproportionately higher exits.
 Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition, indicating burnout risk.
 Younger employees have higher attrition compared to senior employees.
 Low job satisfaction levels are strongly associated with higher attrition.
 HR department shows the lowest attrition, indicating role stability.
